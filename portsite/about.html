About Me

I am a creative who's journey is to expand the knowledge of the my tools so I can can continue to create art to the best of my ability.

I enjoy taking intimidating, complex problems and breaking them down into smaller, simpler pieces.

I'm very resourceful and whenever I run into a seemingly insurmountable problem I try to remember which assets I've forgotten about or haven't tried to solve the problem.

Besides design and coding, my other passions lie with music. I play a variety of instruments, all of which are showcased in the “Music Machine”. Music is similar to coding in that there are an endless amount of possibilities stemming from simple key principles. I try not to spend all my time inside though, and actively participate in seasonal outdoor activities such as mountain biking/climbing in the summer, and skiing/snowboarding during the winter. Also, I drink a lot of tea.
